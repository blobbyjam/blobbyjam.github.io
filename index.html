Manchester United Football Club

Manchester United F.C are team in the Barclays Premier League located in England. As of December 12th, 2014 they are currently in 3rd place in the Barclays Premier League.

Injuries

Recently, they have been hit with injuries to many players which has caused the manager to bring in some youth, and they have done excellent without their normal 1st team players.

Transfers

In the summer, Manchester United signed six new players to their roster:

  • Ángel Di María - Signed for £59.7 million from Real Madrid
  • Luke Shaw - Signed for £30 million from Southampton
  • Daley Blind - Signed for £14 million from Ajax
  • Marcos Rojo - Signed for £16 million from Sporting Clube de Portugal
  • Ander Herrera - Signed for £29 million from Athletic Club
  • Radamel Falcao - Signed on loan from AS Monaco until the end of the season
